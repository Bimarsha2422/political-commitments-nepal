About Us

Overview

Our platform is dedicated to improving transparency and accountability in Nepal. We allow people to track political commitments and ensure that information is accurate and current. By encouraging public participation, we aim to foster a culture of political integrity and active civic engagement.

Submission Portal

Our easy-to-use submission portal allows you to share information about political commitments. You can provide details such as the leader's name, commitment details, supporting evidence, and other relevent details for the commitment.

Verification Process

Submitted information goes through a verification process to check the validity of the evidence. Initially, independent volunteers will handle this, and in the future, we plan to introduce a reputation system to streamline the approval process for trusted contributors.

Future Plans

We plan to introduce a Community Notes feature, where users can provide updates and corrections to commitments. This will help maintain accurate and up-to-date information, with contributions being moderated based on user reputation and community voting.
